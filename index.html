About the Workshop

Autonomous cyber-physical systems (CPS) operate in adversarial and dynamic environments such as smart grids, industrial control systems, and autonomous mobility platforms. As these systems increasingly integrate AI, cybersecurity must evolve from static protection to runtime autonomous defense.

AICR-ACS focuses on security as an autonomic capability, integrating monitoring, analysis, planning, and execution (MAPE-K) into closed-loop adaptive systems. The workshop emphasizes self-protecting and self-healing CPS capable of maintaining operational guarantees under attack.

Topics of Interest

  • AI/ML-based intrusion detection in CPS
  • Reinforcement learning for automated mitigation
  • Security-aware MAPE-K loops
  • Distributed cyber-defense agents
  • Explainable AI for CPS security
  • Post-quantum cryptography
  • Cryptographic agility
  • Cybersecurity digital twins
  • Security of smart grids and industrial IoT
